Our Story

Launched in 1996 as a single establishment in the Queen Victoria Market in the heart of Melbourne, the Drums Cafe specializes in Sri Lankan and Indian cuisine. Our aim is to expose these brilliant cuisines to native Australians as well as tourists all around the world visiting Melbourne. We have been doing exactly this for over 2 decades and have managed to grow significantly with expanding operations.

Come on over and try out our uber-popular Koththu Roti and the delightful Lamprais

Drums Story

Over the years, we have also been involved in a multitude of food markets and festivals. We have attended the International Street Food Festival at the Melbourne Showgrounds as well as the Summer and Winter Wednesday Night Markets at the Queen Victoria Market. Our unique concept of serving Sri Lankan style street food is always a big hit among the crowds.
